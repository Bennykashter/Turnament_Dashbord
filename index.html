Tournament Alignment Dashboard

Quick guide 1. Use the month buttons to filter by time of year — or use the filter row to search by name, location, or tournament size.
2. Sign in with Google (button top-right) to participate.
3. Click any tournament name to log your attendance: Yes / Maybe / No, and add a weapon note.
4. The Rankings tab shows which events the club is leaning toward, sorted by votes.
5. Your response shows under the tournament name in green / amber / red. Click again to update it.
